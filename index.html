Our Vision

Problem Statement

Students at KAU University often struggle with long queues during peak hours, making it difficult to find time for meals between classes. Additionally, there is limited menu visibility, causing uncertainty about available options and wait times.

Project Solution

Our website provides real-time updates on menu availability and estimated wait times. Students can plan their visits better using our scheduling system, ensuring they get their meals without disrupting their academic schedules.
